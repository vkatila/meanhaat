Matarengi Church

Matarengi Church (Swedish: Övertorneå kyrka) is a church in Övertorneå, Norrbotten County, Sweden. The parish’s main church in the town center was built in 1735–1737, and the separate bell tower was completed in 1763. It is one of the best-preserved 18th-century churches in northern Sweden and is home to Sweden’s oldest organs still in use. – Wikipedia –

Lapland View Lodge

Lapland View Lodge is located on Mount Luppio near Övertorneå. Built in harmony with nature, the scenic hotel welcomed its first guests in December 2021 and is now open year-round. Two main buildings with terraces include the reception, restaurant, bar, and a cozy lounge with a fireplace. In the restaurant you can enjoy local cuisine made from Lapland ingredients. The buildings, spa area, and 40 cabins feature large windows offering breathtaking views over the Tornio River Valley and the surrounding landscape. – Lapland View Lodge –
