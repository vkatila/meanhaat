Planes, trains and automobiles

There are several ways you can travel to relatively close main towns such as Kemi, Tornio and Rovaniemi, but getting from those places to the wedding destination is a bit trickier. Here you can read information about some options.

However, if you need any additional assistance with your travels, please feel free to contact us directly!

Flying to Finland

You can fly to Helsinnki and take a connecting flight to either Roovaniemi, Oulu or Kemi-Tornio airports or take a train through Finland to Rovaniemi or Kemi. Kemi is a bit closer to the wedding destination, but you can also get there from Oulu and Rovaniemi by the means described in the sections below.

You can use your favourite online flight search services to find your flights, but we can also help with that. Finnair is flying domestic flights from Helsinki Airport to Rovaniemi, Oulu and Kemi-Tornio. Norwegian also has some flights to Rovaniemi.

Carpool

One of the most convenient and cost efficient ways is to hitch a ride with some of the Finns travelling to the wedding by car. There are several cars with some seats available cruising to the wedding destination from or via all the before mentioned airport and trainstation towns.

We can coordinate carpooling via our wedding WhatsApp group.
