Planes, trains and automobiles

There are several ways you can travel to relatively close main towns such as Kemi, Tornio and Rovaniemi, but getting from those places to the wedding destination can be trickier. Here you can read information about some options. You can also find a map at the end of this page.

Please note that Ylitornio mentioned below is 10km from the church and 20km from the reception venue.

If you need any additional assistance with your travels, please feel free to contact us directly!

Flying to Finland

You can fly to Helsinki and take a connecting flight to either Rovaniemi, Oulu or Kemi-Tornio airports or take a train through Finland to Ylitornio which is at the Finnish/Swedish border close to the wedding venue . From the airports you can also get to the vedding venue by the means described in the sections below.

Ryanair flies from Milan-Bergamo airport to Rovaniemi (eg. on 24 July).

Baltic Air flies from Riga to Oulu (eg. on 24 July).

Flying to Sweden

You can fly to Luleå from Gothenburg and Stockholm. There might be some direct international flights during the summer. From Luleä you can take a train to Haparanda and a bus to the vedding venue.

Trains

There is a direct overnight train from Helsinki to Ylitornio (departure from Helsinki on Friday 24 July at 8:24 PM and arriving to Ylitornio on Saturday 25 July at 9:12 AM). Please note that the times are in Finnish time. If you decide to take the train, bare in mind that due to dynamical pricing, the prices are usually lower the earlier you book the ticket.

You can find tickets from this link -> VR.fi

Busses

There are some direct bus routes from Helsinki to Kemi and Rovaniemi. There are also some busses going to Ylitornio. The timetables of Finnish busses for the summer 2026 will be available by May 2026.

From Haparanda/Tornio main bustation there is a direct Swedish route to Matarengi (Övertorneå) on Saturday 25 July departing at the main bustation at 10:50 AM (Swedish time) and arriving to Matarengi bustation at 12:05 PM (Swedish time).

You can search for timetables and tickets for busses from: Matkahuolto , Onnibussi and Swedish bus schedules

Rental Car

There are several companies offering rental cars that are available for pickup at all airports. You can also have a car delivered to a train station.

for example Hertz Or yoou can also rent a car from a private owners for a more reasonable price from GoMore

Carpool

One of the most convenient and cost efficient ways is to hitch a ride with some of the Finns travelling to the wedding by car. There are several cars with some seats available cruising to the wedding destination from or via all the before mentioned airport and trainstation towns.

We can coordinate carpooling via our wedding WhatsApp group.
